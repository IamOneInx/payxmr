The Problem

Your money is being watched.
Every. Single. Transaction.

Banks, payment apps, and governments can see where you spend, what you earn, and who you pay. That's not how cash used to work.

🏦

Banks can freeze your account

No warning. No recourse. Your money is theirs until they decide otherwise. It happens to real people every day.

👁️

Venmo publishes your purchases

By default, every PayPal and Venmo transaction is public. Your landlord, your boss, anyone — can see what you bought.

📊

Card companies sell your data

Every swipe is a data point sold to advertisers. Your spending habits are a product. You just didn't know you were selling them.

🌍

Sending money abroad is broken

Wire transfers take 3-5 days and charge 5-15% in fees. Monero takes 2 minutes and costs fractions of a cent — anywhere in the world.
